About ThootTrack

ThootTrack is an app that could help small dental clinics that are working with the classic way or at the better case with excel. The inspiration has came from the fact of being a person that is linked to the world of dentistry, I'm preparing my thesis to obtain my doctorate in dental medicine, and by collecting some informations from some installed colleagues that are working on their clinics on the classic way.

This project is a part of my Portfolio Project for Holberton School.

Check out the project repository on GitHub.
