About ThootTrack

ThootTrack is a dental clinic management system designed to help small dental clinics streamline their operations. Inspired by the challenges faced by dental professionals working with traditional methods or using Excel, ThootTrack aims to provide an intuitive and efficient solution.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, developed by a dental medicine student preparing for their doctoral thesis.
